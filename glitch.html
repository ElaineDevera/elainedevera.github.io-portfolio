Lost Identity

Glitch Project

I did this glitch project based on identity and how overtime, sometimes we lose track of ourselves and don't exactly know who we really are or who we are meant to be in society. I chose to glitch these images because sometimes when we lose ourselves, we "glitch out" or in other words, we may not think or act properly which can cause us to feel lost or hopeless.
