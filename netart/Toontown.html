Toontown Online, commonly known as Toontown, was a 3D massively multiplayer online role-playing game based on a cartoon animal world. Players could create characters called "Toons", go into combat, play games, host parties, etc.

Toontown Online was shut down permanently on September 19, 2013. Disney announced the game's closure in August of 2013 due to budget cuts and changes in management at Disney Online. A rewritten version of Toontown has been created here: https://www.toontownrewritten.com/
