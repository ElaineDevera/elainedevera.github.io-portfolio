Nostalgia Games

Net-art

For my netart project, I based it around online kids games that I used to play growing up. I wanted my audience to feel some sort of nostalgia looking back at some of these games and I wanted to share information about these games. Primarily, all these games used to involve adobe flash, which does not exist anymore, therefore I wanted to make my site in honor of it.

Unlocking Your Childhood Memories

Adobe flash may not be avaliable anymore but its legacy will never die as many of us grew up with the software even if we didn't know it. Some of the games listed may still be avaliable but they either have to be downloaded or they have a rewritten version created by unoffical liscensing.

Here's a list of games that I personally enjoyed playing growing up! By clicking on a game's logo, it will take you to its page with more information about the game.
Maybe you'll see something that you've played before and I hope it brings some nostolgia. :)
