Club Penguin was a massively multiplayer online game (MMO), involving a virtual world that contained a range of online games and activities. Players used cartoon penguin-avatars and played in an antarctic-themed open-world.

On January 30, 2017, Club Penguin announced that the current game would be discontinued on March 29, 2017, to make way for its successor, Club Penguin Island.
